I am a front-end developer from Holland, the land of tulips, windmills and

Why work with me? I develop from a strategic perspective of telling stories
through visual and interactive systems. I find great joy in pressing buttons,
but I also take great responsibility for how I press them. Original web design
ideas are sometimes hard to implement, and fast-to-develop websites can
often be not-so-pretty. My role as a pixel pusher is to find the sweet spot
between practical implementation and seamless design.
